Whisper Toggle 2.0.3

Talk to your computer. It types for you - private, local, and reliable.

After install:
  - A mic icon appears in the system tray
  - Press Ctrl+Shift+H to start dictation
  - Speak naturally; local Whisper transcribes on this PC
  - Press Ctrl+Shift+H again to paste final text at the focused cursor

Win+H and live partials are available in Settings, but Ctrl+Shift+H + batch paste is the reliable Windows 11 default.

Works on NVIDIA GPUs (CUDA) and falls back to CPU on Intel Iris (e.g. unhook laptop).
The installer validates the selected local model/device before launch and stops if CUDA/model loading fails.

Your voice never leaves this PC.
