Whisper Toggle 2.0

Talk to your computer. It types for you — live, private, local.

After install:
  • A mic icon appears in the system tray
  • Press Win+H to start dictation (overrides Windows voice typing while running)
  • Speak — text appears as you talk (with a short delay so the mic/GPU catch up)
  • Press Win+H again to finish

Works on NVIDIA GPUs (CUDA) and falls back to CPU on Intel Iris (e.g. unhook laptop).

Your voice never leaves this PC.
